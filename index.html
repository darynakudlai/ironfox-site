About us

We proudly serve Toronto and surrounding areas including Mississauga, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill and many more cities across Ontario.

IRONFOX Reliable Sealing — A Smart Investment in Your Home’s Warmth and Longevity

Canadian winters leave no room for mistakes. Even a small gap can lead to significant heat loss, higher energy bills, moisture intrusion, and long-term structural damage.

Most problems start unnoticed — in facades, concrete joints, windows, or doors — but over time they turn into costly repairs.

IRONFOX provides professional sealing solutions for all types of joints and gaps, both inside and outside your property. We serve residential and commercial buildings using high-performance materials specifically designed for Canada’s harsh climate.

Why choose IRONFOX?
✔ Precise inspection and accurate problem detection
✔ Material selection tailored to each surface
✔ Long-term solutions done right the first time
✔ Protection against moisture, mold, and freezing
✔ Lower heating costs and reduced future repairs
✔ Clean, professional, and durable workmanship

Sealing is not an expense — it is a strategic investment in your property’s energy efficiency and long-term value.

IRONFOX — the confident expert who solves the problem completely and professionally.

Contact us today and protect your home before the next winter season.

Our Services

Windows & Doors

Professional silicone sealing and re-caulking for windows and doors. We eliminate drafts and prevent water intrusion with durable, weather-resistant materials.

Kitchen & Bathroom

Precision mold-resistant silicone application for tiles, showers, bathtubs and sinks — ensuring long-lasting waterproof protection.

Exterior Wall Joints

Advanced sealing for facade panels, concrete joints, siding, gutters and flashing to protect your building from moisture damage.

Glazing

Expert glass-to-metal silicone sealing, curtain wall maintenance and security film installation for structural reliability.

Landscaping

Weather-resistant sealing for driveways, drainage systems, tiled surfaces and steps built to withstand harsh conditions.

Pools & Decorative

High-performance silicone sealing for decorative elements, lighting fixtures, statues and pool structures.
